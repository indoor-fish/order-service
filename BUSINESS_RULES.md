# Order Service — Business Rules

## Rule 1: Cancellation Window
Orders can only be cancelled within **2 hours of creation**. After this window, cancellations must be processed manually through customer support.

## Rule 2: Maximum Order Value
A single order cannot exceed **$50,000** in total value. Orders exceeding this limit are rejected at checkout. High-value orders must be split or handled through an enterprise sales channel.

## Rule 3: Guest Checkout Limit
Unauthenticated (guest) users are limited to **3 orders** before they are required to create an account. This limits anonymous abuse of the ordering system.
